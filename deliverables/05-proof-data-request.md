# Proof Data Request

The structure assumes the strategic claims are approved in principle. These items are needed before final copy, schema, PDFs, and launch.

## Company Identity

- Final public company name: Grable Services or Grable Consulting Services.
- Final logo files: SVG preferred, PNG fallback.
- Brand restrictions, if any.
- Final address, phone numbers, support email, sales email.

## Federal Credentials

- UEI confirmation.
- CAGE confirmation.
- SAM.gov status.
- SDVOSB certificate or verification source.
- NAICS codes.
- Contract vehicles.
- GSA/SEWP status, if any.
- Capability statement source file.
- Any clearance/personnel claims approved for public use.

## Partner And Compliance Claims

- IBM Gold Partner proof.
- IBM reseller / sell authorization proof.
- IBM Maximo Application Suite resale scope.
- IBM Storage resale scope.
- IBM Storage partner badge or approved logo usage.
- Red Hat/AWS/Azure/IBM Cloud partner proof, if used.
- FedRAMP wording that legal/business approves.
- ISO 27001, SOC 2, NIST 800-171, NIST 800-53 proof, if used.
- IL2/IL4/IL5/IL6 wording and limits.

## IBM Storage Services

- IBM Storage product families Grable can resell or support.
- IBM Storage as a Service authorization and quoting path.
- IBM FlashSystem positioning and support scope.
- IBM Storage Defender positioning and support scope.
- Backup, recovery, cyber resilience, and data retention claims approved for public use.
- Storage services delivery model: sizing, procurement, migration, implementation, managed support.
- Any storage certifications, implementation references, or customer examples.

## AI Hosting / AI Infrastructure

- AI hosting offer definition: infrastructure only, managed service, or full implementation.
- Supported AI stack: IBM watsonx, GPU hosting, data platform, storage, backup, monitoring.
- Supported deployment environments: commercial cloud, private cloud, on-prem, GovCloud, or hybrid.
- Security/compliance wording for regulated AI workloads.
- AI hosting performance, GPU, storage, uptime, and support claims approved for public use.
- AI hosting customer proof or internal reference architecture.

## Customer Proof

For each publishable customer:

- Customer name.
- Logo approval.
- Industry.
- Project type.
- Dates or years supported.
- Assets, sqft, users, sites, or systems managed.
- Uptime, cost, timeline, downtime, or productivity metrics.
- Public quote, if available.
- Redaction requirements.

## Offers

- MAS 9 free upgrade offer details.
- Hosting/MaaS tiers, if public.
- Storage Services packages, if public.
- AI Hosting packages, if public.
- Support SLA language.
- Disaster recovery language.
- Security language.
- AC/DC Mobile final name and positioning.

## Conversion Setup

- HubSpot portal details.
- Calendly or scheduling owner.
- Federal lead name and contact.
- Commercial lead name and contact.
- Hosting lead owner.
- MAS 9 upgrade lead owner.
- Storage services lead owner.
- AI hosting lead owner.
- IBM reselling/procurement lead owner.
- RFP/RFI intake destination.
- Newsletter sender identity.
