# Launch Roadmap

## Goal

Move Grable Services from a SEO-dead WordPress brochure to a modern, search-visible IBM infrastructure and Maximo authority site focused on four revenue lanes: **Maximo Hosting / MaaS**, **MAS 9 Upgrade / Migration**, **IBM Storage Services**, and **AI Hosting / AI Infrastructure**. The site should launch quickly with enough structure to expand without rework.

## Launch Strategy

The revamp should ship in layers. Phase 1 must be a credible production site, not a placeholder. Later phases deepen content, tools, case studies, and CMS workflows.

## Phase 1 - Foundation Launch

**Objective:** Launch the new site with the highest-value commercial and federal paths live, led by hosting, MAS 9 upgrade, storage services, and AI hosting.

**Core pages:**

- `/`
- `/services/`
- `/services/mas9-migration/`
- `/services/maximo-hosting/`
- `/services/maximo-on-fedramp/`
- `/services/storage-services/`
- `/services/ai-hosting/`
- `/services/ibm-maximo-reselling/`
- `/federal/`
- `/industries/`
- `/industries/life-sciences/`
- `/products/ibm-storage-services/`
- `/products/acdc-mobile/`
- `/resources/`
- `/company/about/`
- `/contact/`
- `/legal/privacy/`

**Required launch features:**

- One semantic H1 per page.
- Sitewide searchable/indexable robots policy.
- `/sitemap.xml`, `/robots.txt`, `/llms.txt`.
- 301 redirects for all current WordPress URLs.
- Organization, WebSite, Service, BreadcrumbList, and FAQ JSON-LD where applicable.
- Top credential strip with UEI, CAGE, SDVOSB, IBM Gold, FedRAMP-aligned claims marked `[VERIFY]`.
- Audience-aware CTA language for Federal and Commercial visitors.
- Contact form with routing fields for Hosting, MAS 9 Upgrade, Storage Services, AI Hosting, Federal, Commercial, Support, and RFP/RFI.
- IBM reselling and storage claims sourced from verified Partner Plus / reseller authorization records before publication.

## Phase 2 - Authority Content

**Objective:** Win priority search and AI-citation surfaces.

**Content to publish:**

- MAS 9 Migration Playbook page.
- FedRAMP Maximo April 2026 explainer.
- Maximo as a Service definition page.
- IBM Storage as a Service guide.
- AI hosting for regulated workloads guide.
- Maximo AppPoints and procurement guide.
- 6-8 FAQ-rich supporting posts.
- 2 publishable case studies.
- Capability statement landing page and PDF.

## Phase 3 - Lead Engines

**Objective:** Add interactive conversion assets competitors do not have.

**Tools:**

- MAS 9 Readiness Assessment.
- Hosting TCO Calculator.
- Storage sizing / resilience checklist.
- AI hosting readiness assessment.
- FedRAMP Cost Estimator.
- License Optimization Estimator.

**CRM:**

- HubSpot list segmentation by audience.
- HubSpot routing by primary buying motion: Hosting, MAS 9 Upgrade, Storage Services, AI Hosting, IBM Reselling, Federal RFP/RFI.
- Federal and commercial MQL routing.
- Capability statement download workflow.
- RFP/RFI intake workflow.

## Phase 4 - CMS And Scale

**Objective:** Let the business publish without engineering bottlenecks.

**CMS models:**

- Service pillar.
- Service subpage.
- Industry.
- Case study.
- Blog post.
- Whitepaper.
- Tool.
- Author.
- FAQ.
- Navigation.

**Governance:**

- Quarterly update process for `/llms.txt`, capability statement, credentials, and federal claims.
- Quarterly review process for IBM reseller authorization, IBM Storage claims, FedRAMP wording, AI hosting claims, and partner badge usage.
- Monthly blog/content cadence.
- Case study approval workflow.

## Launch Readiness Checklist

- All `[VERIFY]` claims reviewed.
- No `noindex,nofollow` on production.
- Redirects tested.
- Forms tested.
- Mobile navigation tested.
- Accessibility pass on keyboard, contrast, focus states, and screen-reader landmarks.
- Lighthouse checks for LCP, INP, CLS, SEO, and accessibility.
